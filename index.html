About Riwaaz Jewelry

From Kakrahwa Bazar to Your Collection

Welcome to Riwaaz, where tradition meets contemporary elegance.

Our journey began on Police Chowki Road in Kakrahwa Bazar. In a small town where every piece of jewelry tells a story of heritage and celebration, we saw an opportunity to bring high-quality, stunning imitation jewelry to those who appreciate the finer things in life—without the traditional price tag.

The Siblings Behind the Brand

Riwaaz isn't just a business; it's a family legacy. Founded and operated by three siblings, we combine our unique strengths to bring you a curated collection that reflects our passion for craftsmanship.

Nandita Verma — Founder The visionary who dreamed of making luxury accessible. Her eye for design and commitment to quality are the heart of every collection.
Baljee Verma — CEO The driving force behind our growth, ensuring that Riwaaz stays true to its mission of excellence and customer trust.
Aayush Verma — MD The hands-on leader managing our daily operations, ensuring that every piece that leaves our town reaches you in perfect condition.

Our Philosophy

The word Riwaaz means "Tradition." We believe that jewelry is more than just an accessory—it is a way to express your identity and carry forward the beauty of our culture.

Whether it is a grand wedding or a simple everyday look, we strive to provide imitation jewelry that feels as special as the moments you wear it in. From our home in Kakrahwa Bazar to your doorstep, we are proud to share a piece of our world with you.
